Practical For Employability Skill Enhancement (PESE)

WEEK 1: Introduction to Course ▼
  • The difference between soft skills and technical skills.
  • Understanding that 40% of professional time is spent in communication.
  • The critical importance of effective time management in the industry.
WEEK 2: Self Awareness (SWOC + Johari Window) ▼
  • Analyzing Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Challenges.
  • Utilizing the Johari Window for better interpersonal understanding.
  • The importance of self-awareness in personal and professional growth.
WEEK 3: Presentation Skills ▼
  • Learning to know and read your audience effectively.
  • How to logically and engagingly structure presentation content.
  • Mastering body language and non-verbal communication cues.
WEEK 4: Effective Writing ▼
  • Applying the ABC rule: Aim, Brevity, and Clarity.
  • Learning how to be concise without losing the core message.
  • Drafting professional emails and technical documentation.
WEEK 5: Group Discussion ▼
  • Using the 5W1H method (Who, What, When, Where, Why, How) to structure arguments.
  • The dynamics of teamwork and active listening.
  • Displaying leadership, confidence, and respectful disagreement.
